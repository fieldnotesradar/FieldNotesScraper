On Campus 

Located right in the heart of more than twenty American college towns, Graduate Hotels is inspired by and tailored to the schools and communities it serves. With a bordering-on-obsessive attention to local detail and history, the hotels are, like the college experience itself, youthful, optimistic, and fun. 

Before we even met the Graduate crew, we admired the care that they put into the research and design of their spaces and communications. So, when they reached out to create a Field Notes edition to be offered to their guests, we were all in, right away. These 3-Packs are available at every Graduate Hotel and are also available for purchase online here, at Field Notes.

The photos were taken by Craig Shimala in Seattle and Berkeley. The covers are glossy on the exterior and matte inside, and the inside 48 pages are ruled, perfect for lecture notes and assignments, or tailgate, playlist, or road trip planning. Silver staples hold the whole package together.

Graduate Hotels has locations from coast to coast. Find one near you.

SPECIFICATIONS:
  • 01.

    Proudly printed by the good people of eDOC Communications, Mount Prospect, Ill.

  • 02.

    Cover: WestRock Tango C1S 12pt “White,” with a thick, brute force application of Pantone soy-based Toyo inks.
